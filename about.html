About Us

Frooti Smoothie was founded on a simple idea — that fresh, thoughtfully sourced ingredients make a difference. In flavor. In health. In the world. We believe that what you put in your body should be real and crafted with care. Fresh is better and with 200 locaitons across the US, there will always be a Frooti Smoothie waiting for you. It’s not always the easy way, but it’s the Frooti way — and it tastes just right.
